physics , maths

dot product

  • perpendicular/orthogonal components are 0
    • $\hat{i} \cdot \hat{j} = \hat{j} \cdot \hat{k} = \hat{k} \cdot \hat{i}$ and so on and so forth

vector product

  • I generally find the right hand rule to be very straight forward
    • index $\times$ middle = thumb
