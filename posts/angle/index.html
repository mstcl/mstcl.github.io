Solid angles

2021-07-01
maths
  • unit: steradian (sr)
  • analogous to plane angle, which is defined by arc length and radius, but unlike the plane angle, it is defined by the surface area of a sphere subtended by the lines and by the radius of that sphere (squared, for a correct dimensional analysis.
    • $\theta = \frac{l}{dr}$
    • $\Omega = \frac{a}{r^2}$
    • unlike the plane angle, it cannot be conceived of without reference to an arbitrary sphere.
