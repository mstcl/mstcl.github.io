The loss-of-precision theorem

2021-07-18
compphys

Subtraction

If b and c are positive normalized floating-point binary machine numbers with $b < c$ and

$$ 2^{-r} \leq 1 - \frac{c}{b} \leq 2^{-s}, $$

Then at most $r$ and at least $s$ significant binary bits are lost in the subtraction $b-c$.
